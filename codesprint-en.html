Web Experience Toolkit CodeSprint

Where:
1125@Carleton — 5th Floor
When:
August 14 and 15, 1:00 pm to 4:00 pm

Join us in the afternoons for a Web Experience Toolkit CodeSprint and a fun competition.

You don't have to know any coding to participate! We're looking for people from various disciplines, such as UX designers, testers, communicators, technical writers and implementers.

We want you to bring your expertise and fresh perspectives on user-centered problem-solving, such as improving various Web Experience Toolkit components to make them more user-friendly.

If you're new to Web Experience Toolkit, we'll walk you through the process.

Competition

A fun and friendly competition will be held as part of the Web Experience Toolkit CodeSprint. Rise to the top of the leaderboard by claiming "bounties" and earning bonuses.

Details

  • Starts: August 14, 1:00 pm (Eastern Daylight Time)
  • Ends: August 15, 4:00 pm (Eastern Daylight Time)
  • Open to everyone, including people from various disciplines and skill levels.
  • Active GitHub account required.
  • Competitors may participate remotely. They do not need to be present at the CodeSprint or even CodeFest.
  • Registration is automatic. Competitors will be added to the leaderboard when points are first awarded.
  • Points will be awarded for claiming "bounties" and earning bonuses.
  • Points awarded to competitors will be displayed on the leaderboard.
  • Points for "bounties" will only be awarded to the first individual or team to complete the task. Awarded points may be spread over multiple individuals but the total points awarded must not exceed the points identified for that task.
  • Bonuses for a task can be awarded multiple times (once for each time the task is completed).
  • New "bounties" and bonuses may be added during the competition at the discretion of the competition supervisors.
  • Points will only be awarded where there is a verifiable record on GitHub (e.g., merged pull request, issue filed on GitHub) with a timestamp that falls between the start and end times of the competition (between August 14 at 1:00 pm and August 15 at 4:00 pm, Eastern Daylight Time).
  • All competition updates, including a history of points awarded, will be posted to the competition updates page.
  • Report unrewarded points, incorrect awarding of points and suggestions for new "bounties" and bonuses to the competition supervisors or the CodeSprint competition tracking issue on GitHub.
